# Collections

## The Crate Institute of Design

The Collections directory contains the active archival collections of The Crate Institute of Design.

Each folder represents a major area of study within the Institute.

Collections are not isolated departments. Artifacts may belong to multiple collections when appropriate.

## Collection Areas

- Typography
- Publications
- Record Culture
- Skate Culture
- Industrial Design
- Packaging
- Signage
- Digital Products
- Photography
- Identity Systems
- Transportation Systems
- Object Studies

Collection status: Active  
Archive status: Incomplete
