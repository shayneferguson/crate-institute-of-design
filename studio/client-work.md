**Client Work**  
  
**Applied Research**  
  
The Studio views client work as a form of applied research.  
  
Each project presents an opportunity to study communication, systems, behavior, and design in practice.  
  
⸻  
  
**Contribution to the Archive**  
  
Completed projects may contribute observations, methodologies, and insights to the Institute’s archive.  
  
The objective is not to archive clients.  
  
The objective is to preserve knowledge generated through the work.  
  
⸻  
  
**Mutual Benefit**  
  
Clients receive thoughtful design solutions.  
  
The Institute gains a deeper understanding of how design functions in the world.  
  
This relationship strengthens both the Studio and the Archive.  
  
⸻  
  
**Closing Statement**  
  
The Studio exists to apply the principles of The Crate Institute of Design to real-world challenges.  
  
Research informs the work.  
  
The work informs future research.  
  
Together they form a continuous cycle of observation, understanding, design, and documentation.
