# The Crate Institute of Design  
  
The Crate Institute of Design is a design research studio and archival institution dedicated to the collection, study, preservation, documentation, and exploration of design.  
  
The Institute operates through two interconnected divisions:  
  
## Institute  
  
Research, archives, collections, publications, and documentation.  
  
## Studio  
  
Applied design practice including identity systems, digital experiences, publications, and design systems.
