# Identity Excavation Framework™

> Used during the research phase to uncover the truth already present within an organization.

---

## When It's Used

Phase 01 (Observe) of [[the-crate-method]].

## Purpose

To uncover the truth already present within an organization — not to impose an identity from the outside, but to excavate what already exists.

## Key Questions

1. What exists?
2. What matters?
3. What is misunderstood?
4. What should be preserved?
5. What should evolve?

## Underlying Principle

Every organization already has an identity. The work is discovery, not invention. The framework guides researchers to surface what's real rather than project what seems appealing.
