# Foundational Institute Studies

> Internal studies developed before client work — proof of thinking, methodology, and execution.

---

## Purpose

Before taking on clients, the Institute develops internal studies. These projects demonstrate methodology in practice and build a portfolio of thinking.

---

## Active Studies

### Study 001 — The Crate Institute of Design
Designing the Institute itself. The studio's own identity is Study 001.

### Study 002 — Rhythm Research
Building a cultural research division. An internal division exploring culture as a lens for brand and identity work.

### Study 003 — Loft28
Creating a productized service from the ground up.

### Study 004 — Brand Archaeology: Zoo York
Investigating institutional storytelling and artifact-based branding.

### Study 005 — Brand Archaeology: Design Annuals
Studying how editorial systems communicate identity over time.

---

## What These Studies Prove

- The Institute can apply its own methodology to itself (Study 001)
- Research can extend into culture and artifacts, not just organizations (Studies 002, 004, 005)
- The methodology works for building new things, not just clarifying existing ones (Study 003)
