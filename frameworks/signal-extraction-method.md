# Signal Extraction Method™

> Used during the clarity phase to separate what's real from what's noise.

---

## When It's Used

Phase 02 (Interpret) and Phase 03 (Clarify) of [[the-crate-method]].

## Purpose

Reveal the essential identity beneath complexity by distinguishing:

**Noise (remove):**
- Assumptions
- Noise
- Trends

**Signal (keep):**
- Truths
- Strengths
- Differentiation

## Goal

Strip away what has accumulated (trends followed, messaging borrowed, assumptions never examined) and surface what is actually true and distinctive about the organization.

See also: [[identity-excavation-framework]], [[the-crate-method]]
