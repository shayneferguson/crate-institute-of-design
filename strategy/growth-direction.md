# Growth Direction

> Where The Crate Institute of Design is headed — goals, bets, and strategic priorities.

## Long-Term Vision

The Crate Institute of Design exists to help organizations better understand themselves.

Through research, clarity, systems, and culture, the Institute transforms complexity into understanding — and understanding into meaningful experiences.

The goal is not simply to create brands. The goal is to reveal identity.

## What We're Saying No To

- Trend-chasing visual refreshes
- Clients who want design without strategy
- Fast, cheap, transactional engagements
- Being categorized as a "branding agency" or "web studio"

---

## Strategic Clarity (non-negotiable)

- Research before design — always
- Services are studies, audits, and systems — not isolated deliverables
- We identify as an Institute, not an agency or studio
