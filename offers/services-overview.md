# Services Overview

> What The Crate Institute of Design offers, how engagements are structured, and the value delivered.

---

## Service Architecture Philosophy

The Crate Institute approaches identity as a research problem before it becomes a design problem.

Services are structured as **studies, audits, and systems** — not isolated creative deliverables.

---

## Service Lines

### 1. Identity Research Audit

**Purpose:** Understand the organization before making recommendations.

**Deliverables:**
- Stakeholder interviews
- Brand audit
- Competitor review
- Communication audit
- Research findings report
- Strategic recommendations

**Outcome:** A clear understanding of where the organization is today and where opportunities for alignment exist.

---

### 2. Identity Systems Study

**Purpose:** Transform research into a cohesive identity system.

**Deliverables:**
- Positioning framework
- Messaging framework
- Identity system
- Typography direction
- Visual language
- Design standards

**Outcome:** A documented identity system capable of supporting future growth.

---

### 3. Digital Experience Program

**Purpose:** Translate identity into experience.

**Deliverables:**
- Website architecture
- Content strategy
- UX direction
- Design system implementation
- Launch support

**Outcome:** A digital presence aligned with organizational identity.

---

## Process Philosophy

Services follow the sequence: Research → Audit → Strategy → Identity → Digital Experience.

Each stage builds on the previous. Design does not begin until the research and strategy phases are complete.
