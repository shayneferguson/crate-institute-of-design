# Ideal Client

> Who The Crate Institute of Design is built to serve — their profile, goals, frustrations, and buying triggers.

---

## Client Profile

Founders, businesses, organizations, and creative professionals who:
- Care deeply about what they do
- Are often experts in their field
- Have invested years developing their craft, refining their services, and building valuable businesses

## The Core Pattern

Their work has evolved. Their brand has not.

They are not looking for trends. They are not looking for shortcuts. They are looking for clarity, direction, and a visual identity that accurately reflects who they are.

## What They're Experiencing (Pain Points)

- Their website no longer represents them
- Their messaging feels fragmented
- Their visual identity has been assembled piece by piece over time
- Their marketing feels inconsistent
- Their communication lacks cohesion
- They know their work is valuable — they simply struggle to express that value in a way that others immediately understand

## What They Actually Want (Goals)

- Confidence
- Consistency
- A clear sense of identity
- Every customer touchpoint to feel intentional
- A brand that feels coherent and trustworthy
- Most importantly: their outward appearance to finally match the quality of their work

## Buying Trigger

The gap between how good their work actually is and how their brand represents it becomes undeniable. Often triggered by: a missed opportunity, a referral who chose someone else, a website they're embarrassed to share, or a growth phase that demands more credibility.

## Green Flags (good fit signals)

- Deep expertise in their field
- Willing to do the research and reflection work
- Understand that design is an investment, not a cost
- Have something real to say — we just help them say it clearly

## Red Flags (bad fit signals)

- Looking for quick, trend-based visual refresh
- Not willing to go through a discovery/strategy phase
- Expect design to substitute for unclear thinking
- Price-shopping
